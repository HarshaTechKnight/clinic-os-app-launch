How it works

Walk-in to walk-out, reimagined

01

Step 01

Patient walks in

Assistant enters a name or phone number. Returning patients auto-fill — name, history, everything. New patients are registered in under 10 seconds. A token is assigned instantly.

02

Step 02

Queue runs itself

The live queue updates automatically. Patients receive SMS alerts as their turn approaches. Step-outs, priority changes, and pauses are handled with a single tap.

03

Step 03

Doctor consults with full context

Complete patient thread on screen — past visits, diagnoses, prescriptions. The doctor focuses on care, not paperwork. Bill is generated in one tap at the end.

04

Step 04

Data that drives growth

Every consultation feeds into analytics — patient volume, revenue trends, peak hours, consultation times. For the first time, the clinic can make decisions backed by real data.
