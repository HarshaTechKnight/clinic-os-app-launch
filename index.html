About ClinicOS

Built for Vijayawada Clinics

ClinicOS is a smart clinic management system designed specifically for small clinics, nursing homes, and individual practitioners across Andhra Pradesh.

We reduce patient registration time from 5 minutes to just 10 seconds. Our automated queue management sends SMS alerts to patients in Telugu and English — so your waiting room stays calm and your staff stays focused.

How It Works

From Registration to Consultation

4 simple steps that replace a day's worth of paperwork — running on your existing Android phone.

1
📱

Register Patient

Receptionist enters the patient's phone number. Returning patients load instantly. New patients registered in under 10 seconds.

⚡ Under 10 seconds
2
🎫

Get Token

Automatic token generated and displayed on the clinic screen. Patient receives SMS with token number and estimated wait time.

📲 Instant SMS in Telugu
3
💬

SMS Alert

Patient gets WhatsApp/SMS: "Your turn in 5 mins!" They can wait outside, have tea, rest — and return exactly when needed.

🔔 Zero missed turns
4
🩺

Consult & Bill

Doctor consults with full patient history visible. Receptionist generates GST bill in one tap. Queue auto-advances to next token.

✅ Zero paper, zero confusion
